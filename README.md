 Career-Guide
Career Guide is an interactive decision-support tool designed to help students choose the right academic stream and career path based on their interests, aptitude, and personality traits. It combines structured questionnaires, scoring algorithms, and personalized recommendations to make career planning easier and more accurate.

Purpose
The tool aims to:
Help students- understand their strengths and abilities
- Suggest suitable streams (Science, Commerce, Arts)
- Generate personalized career recommendations
- Reduce confusion while choosing future study paths
- Provide a clear roadmap for growth and successful career planning

Goals
-Reduce confusion while selecting future career paths
-Give accurate, structured guidance
-Make career planning accessible and easy for all students
